Minecraft Sculpture - Lechon

The prompt for this project was to build something of cultural significance and have that sculpture 3D printed. I wanted to base my project off of Filipino culture, specifically the food. I built a model of lechon, or roasted pig, which is often only eaten at grand celebrations, given its large weight, size, and high cost. Along with the models is an image of my bed.
