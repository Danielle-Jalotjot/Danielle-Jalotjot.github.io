Net Art - When I Get Home

With the help of Twine, an interactive program for story-telling, I created my project, When I Get Home, a short piece that navigates the complications of getting home safely. I intentionally created a simpler plot to focus on the emotions that one may feel: fear, worry, caution, etc, when alone at night.
