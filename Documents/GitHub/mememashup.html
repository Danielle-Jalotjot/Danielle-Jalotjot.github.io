Meme Mashup - Asian Parents

Although memes are intended to be comical, many are both dark and funny. I attempted this in my Asian Parents meme mashup, using the old Asian father meme, in which the text paired with it is usually scolding and demeaning the child. The other incorporated meme is the more recent Draw 25, which is more lighthearted. Combining these two images created a more somber, yet comical take on my experience with Asian parenting.
