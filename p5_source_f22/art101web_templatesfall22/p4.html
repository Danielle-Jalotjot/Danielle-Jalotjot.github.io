Project 4

For the final project, I chose to explore the ideas of skeuomorphism and flat design in UI. Like Sketch 7, I implemented the two required p5.js libraries: p5.scenemanager and p5.sound. For the third library of choice, I included p5.clickable, which is described as an "event driven, easy-to-use button library." Conceptually, my final project follows the progression of skeuomorphism and the shift to flat design, concluding with my interpretation of a combined aesthetic of both designs. By drawing the majority of the design layouts, I felt I successfully created unique scenes that effectively portray skeuomorphism and flat design. Due to time, I was unable to include a second, more modern skeuomorphic scene as mentioned in my final proposal. Nonetheless, I am very proud with how my final project came out!
