To begin drawing, here are the keyboard controls:

1 - thin black line
q - thick black line
2 - thin white line
w - thick white line
3 - random brush stroke
4 - large, dynamic colorful ellipses
5 - small, static neutral ellipses
6 - yellow brush stroke image
7 - red brush stroke image
8 - blue brush stroke image
9 - erase tool

x - clear drawing | p - save drawing as png
