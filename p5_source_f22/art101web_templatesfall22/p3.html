Sketch 6

Here is a pixel-style representation of a starry night sky, created using three grid arrays. One grid array creates the stars, the second text grid array creates the moon, and the third text grid array creates the clouds. To add variety in the position, scale, color, style, and rotation for the stars, I created custom functions for each new colored star and placed them in the draw loop, where I then altered their position, scale, and rotation. Overall, I really enjoyed creating this imagery, and if I were to improve upon it, I would simply add more to it, such as a cityscape at the foreground by increasing the canvas size.

Project 3

Resembling a conventional clock, I chose to set alarms for when I should water my 2 plants: one requiring watering every two days, and the other requiring watering only once a week. In this clock system, I replaced the hands of the clock with colorful text, and set the backgound to correlate with the time of day. At the bottom is also a visualization for the day of the month, so the length of the green bar correlates to the day of the month. I believe I have successfully created a visualization for the specific routine in my life of watering my plants. To improve or add to this project, I would add background music or more sound effects.
